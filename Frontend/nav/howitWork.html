How to Use the Relaxing Sound Mixer

1. Exploring Sound Options

Click on the sound icons (e.g., rain, ocean, fireplace) to start or stop each sound. Multiple sounds can play together to create a personalized mix.

2. Adjusting Sound Levels

Click on the slider icon next to each sound to reveal a volume slider. Adjust the slider to control the volume of each sound individually.

3. Creating Playlists

Click on the "Playlist" button to open a menu with different music categories like Sleep, Productivity, or Relax. Select a category, and a random track will start playing.

4. Saving Favorites

After creating a sound mix, click the "Save Favorite" button to store your current mix. Your favorites will appear in the Favorites section, allowing easy access in the future.

5. Managing Favorites

Click on any saved favorite to instantly recreate the sound mix. To delete a favorite, click the "Delete" button next to it.

6. Admin Panel (Optional)

Admins can add new sound categories, upload tracks, and manage existing playlists directly from the admin panel.

Enjoy Your Personalized Relaxing Experience!

Mix and match sounds to create your perfect atmosphere for focus, relaxation, or sleep. Happy listening!
